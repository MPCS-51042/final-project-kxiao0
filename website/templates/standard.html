How to use

1. Numbers

x/y: x = # of base items you have; y = # of base items you need

2. Color coding

0/2 : meaning you still need more of this type of base item

1/1 : meaning that for this base item, you got what's needed

1/1 : meaning that for this base item, you got MORE than what's needed

3. Abbreviation

  • BFS: B.F. Sword
  • CV: Chain Vest
  • GB: Giant's Belt
  • NLR: Needlessly Large Rod
  • NC: Negatron Cloak
  • RB: Recurve Bow
  • SG: Sparring Gloves
  • S: Spatula
  • TG: Tear of the Goddess

4. Ramp up

  • Slow Roll: 3-star units at level 5 by rolling your excess gold every round.
  • Standard: Flexible strategy focused around a healthy economy while still keeping up in levels. General rules are level 6 by 3-2, level 7 by 4-1, and level 8 by 5-1
  • Fast 8: Level up to 8 aggressively, usually by round 4-3, to find 4 and 5-cost champions and unlock powerful synergies before other players
